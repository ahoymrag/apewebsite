Our Vision Statement

Ape is a framework for Web3 Python applications and designing smart contracts.  With advanced functionality for testing, deployment, and on-chain interactions, we take the guesswork out of building by packaging the best tools in the industry for an all-in-one experience. 

With Ape, you can use the full power of the Ethereum platform from your Python programs: create and verify transactions, interact with contracts, and communicate with peers. Ape can be used as a package or as a standalone executable.

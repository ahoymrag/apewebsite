modular plugins

Developing custom smart contracts for Ethereum can be confusing. Most developers use 2-3 platforms to build and deploy their smart contracts.  We make development smoother with our modular approach. We have created a complete set of tools and frameworks to assist in the testing, deployment, and creation of tokens, markets, and oracles that connect smart contracts with web3.
